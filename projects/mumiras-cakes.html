Mumira's Cakes

2025 • Cake Business E-commerce Platform • In Development

Problem Tackled

I built this project to create a comprehensive e-commerce platform for a custom cake business. The goal was to streamline the ordering process, showcase cake designs, and manage customer orders efficiently while providing a beautiful user experience.

Target Users/Market

Cake businesses and customers looking for custom cake orders, with a focus on providing an intuitive ordering system, beautiful cake galleries, and efficient order management for both customers and business owners.

Challenges Faced

Creating an intuitive cake customization interface; implementing a robust order management system; designing a visually appealing gallery of cake designs; integrating payment processing for custom orders; ensuring responsive design for mobile users.

Successes Achieved

Successfully created a modern e-commerce platform with cake customization features, order management system, and beautiful UI/UX design. Implemented responsive design and integrated payment processing for seamless customer experience.

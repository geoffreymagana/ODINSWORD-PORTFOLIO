Problem Tackled

I built this to showcase custom artworks, designs, and merch in a clean, client-friendly format. The goal was to create a professional portfolio website that effectively displays artistic work while maintaining excellent user experience and performance.

Target Users/Market

Artists, freelance designers, and digital creators who need a professional online presence to showcase their work, attract clients, and sell their creations through an elegant and user-friendly platform.

Challenges Faced

Image quality vs performance, setting up live previews for art variants; creating a gallery system that handles high-resolution images efficiently; designing an interface that works well for both browsing and purchasing artwork.

Successes Achieved

Clean layout with scroll-based interactions and gallery structure. Successfully designed a portfolio system that balances visual appeal with functionality, creating an engaging platform for showcasing artistic work.
